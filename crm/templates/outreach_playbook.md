# Cold outreach playbook (short)

## Rules
- Short subject + short body after first experiments
- No voice pitch on first touch (lead response / office helper)
- 5–10 personalized emails/day
- BCC owner on outbound; CRM-first (don’t rescrape every batch)
- Batch N = **new** companies; follow-up = day-N bump to **non-replies only**

## Subject ideas
- `{Company} — missed leads?`
- `Free setup for {Company}`
- `15 min for {Company}?`

## Short body
```
Hi {Company} team,

Local trades lose jobs when leads sit. I set up a free AI office helper
for faster lead reply (forms/after-hours). First few shops free while I prove it.

Open to a quick look this week? Reply here and I’ll send details.

{YOUR_NAME}
```

## Status flow
`new` → `contacted` → `replied` → `qualified` | `won_pilot` | `lost`
