# Roofer GTM outreach (sell AI employee pilots)

## Positioning

You are not selling “chatbots.” You are offering a **free working AI office employee** so the roofer stops losing jobs to slow lead response. Monthly maintain is optional after they see value.

## Nomenclature

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Batch N** | Emails to **new** companies not yet contacted |
| **Follow-up** | Day-N bump to **non-replies** from a prior batch |
| Ops CRM | skill `roofer-lead-crm` |

## Default copy = SHORT (user correction)

Long baseline subject was Batch 1 only. Prefer short subjects:
`{Company} — missed leads?` · `Free setup for {Company}` · `15 min for {Company}?`

```
Hi {Company} team,

Local roofers lose jobs when leads sit. I set up a free AI office helper
for faster lead reply (forms/after-hours). First few shops free while I
prove it.

15 min this week? WhatsApp {OwnerPhone} or reply here.

{Name}
```

**Do not** lead with voice agents, Vapi, or “AI phone receptionist” unless asked.

## Batch checklist

- [ ] Agent Mail ACTIVE; inbox known  
- [ ] Test email received by owner  
- [ ] Geo locked; CRM has `new`+email (or bulk scrape first)  
- [ ] Short subject/body + variant logged  
- [ ] BCC owner; 5–10/day cap  
- [ ] Schedule follow-up for non-replies (~day 3) if requested  

## After reply

1. Notify owner on Telegram  
2. Draft reply; **CC** owner  
3. Scope free pilot: lead response first; voice/SMS only if they want it  
4. Implementation uses phased checklist + custom Python preference
